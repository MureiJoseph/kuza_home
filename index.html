The Curriculum

Your 8-Week Roadmap

A comprehensive journey mapped out to transition you from an individual contributor to an empowered leader.

Week 1: The Self

Developing your personal Leadership Creed. Understanding emotional intelligence (EQ) and shifting from a "doing" mindset to a "managing" mindset.

Week 2: The Team Dynamics

Mastering the art of building authentic trust. Exploring the "Ubuntu Leader" archetype and evaluating your team's power dynamics.

Week 3: Radical Candor & Feedback

Applying the SBI Feedback Model. Overcoming the "Reluctant Authority" trap by delivering direct, empathetic, and actionable feedback.

Week 4: 3-Directional Leadership™

Learning how to effectively lead DOWN (your team), UP (your manager), and ACROSS (your peers and stakeholders).

Week 5: Delegation & The DACI Framework

Moving beyond task assignment. Using the DACI Decision-Making Framework to empower your team and reclaim your strategic time.

Week 6: Strategic Execution

Aligning daily team operations with high-level corporate goals. Setting KPIs that measure true impact, not just activity.

Week 7: Conquering Imposter Syndrome

Reviewing psychometric data to identify personal blind spots. Strategies to build unshakeable leadership confidence.

Week 8: The Capstone & Bridge Crossing

Finalizing your transition. Presenting your impact plan and officially crossing "The Manager Bridge".

Our Approach

3-Directional Leadership™

Most programmes only teach managers to lead DOWN. We teach them to lead in all three directions—because that's what real leadership requires.

DOWN

Your Team

  • Building authentic trust
  • Effective delegation
  • Radical candor feedback
  • Developing your people

UP

Your Manager

  • Managing expectations
  • Getting resources & support
  • Increasing visibility
  • Strategic alignment

ACROSS

Your Peers

  • Influence without authority
  • Cross-functional decisions
  • Navigating politics
  • Building alliances
